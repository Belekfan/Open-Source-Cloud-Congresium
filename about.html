About Project

This is a website containing links to the event pages of global firms and organizations, and it continues to be updated.

Purpose Of The Project

In this project, the congresses, seminars, and similar events of global firms and organizations are brought together through a simple web interface, aiming to allow users to easily discover and participate in events that match their interests.
