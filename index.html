Defence Industry Events

Defense industry companies engage in activities such as the design, production, and sale of military technology and equipment. These firms develop and supply products like defense weaponry, vehicles, equipment, and communication systems to defense and security forces. As a strategically important sector, the defense industry plays a significant role in national security and defense policies, typically regulated and overseen by governments.
